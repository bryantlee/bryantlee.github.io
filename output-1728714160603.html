  • Scoring System
    • Define a set evaluation methodology and scoring rubric to ensure a consistent assessment process.

  • Current Challenges
    • Existing systems are cumbersome and inefficient.
    • Current processes lack automation, leading to time-consuming manual tasks.
    • There is difficulty in extracting actionable insights from data.
  • Objectives
    • To enhance business efficiency and streamline workflows through improved technology.
    • Increase automation to reduce manual processing errors.
    • Facilitate better decision-making through comprehensive and accessible data insights.

    • User-friendly interfaces for ease of adoption across teams.
    • Integration with existing data management systems.
  • User Roles and Permissions
    • Design custom roles with tailored access as per organizational requirements.
    • Implement secure user management and authentication protocols.
