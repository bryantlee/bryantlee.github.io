Passive Looking

  • Purpose
    • To gather requirements for new or replacement software solutions.
    • Provide clarity and alignment on software needs and objectives.
    • Identify key criteria for software evaluation and selection.
  • Scope
    • Covers the full cycle from software selection to deployment.
    • Includes integration and compatibility evaluation.
    • Focuses on improving efficiency and addressing current challenges.
  • Background
    • Current solutions have limitations and need to be reassessed.
    • Aim to improve performance and introduce new capabilities.
    • Consideration of replacement or complement to existing systems.
