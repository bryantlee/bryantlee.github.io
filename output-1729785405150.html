To achieve this, the system asks thoughtful questions tailored to the subject matter of each section, using existing information to generate new, relevant questions. The system assists in modifying and organizing content to ensure consistency and completeness throughout the requirements document. The ultimate goal is to help stakeholders make informed decisions, improve the quality of the requirements specification, and reduce manual effort and potential errors.
