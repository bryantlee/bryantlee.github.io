  • Purpose
    • Implement a no SQL database solution to meet current and future data needs.
    • Support organizational shift towards cloud-based solutions.
    • Facilitate improved data management and scalability.
  • Scope
    • Acquire and deploy a no SQL database in the cloud.
    • Ensure seamless integration with existing systems as needed.
    • Provide necessary support and training for effective use.
  • Background
    • Current reliance on Redis requires a cloud-based upgrade to meet evolving data needs.
    • The organization anticipates growth and requires scalable database solutions.
    • Seeking complementary solution to enhance current technology stack.
